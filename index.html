How it began...

Alec and Maddy first met in college at West Virginia University in 2019. These two love birds were once no more than coworkers. Maddy first caught Alec's eye in the Kroger where they both worked part time. He was far from shy about approaching her, but Maddy's shy personality came off as cold and Alec was discouraged.

Despite Maddy seeming uninterested, Alec persisted. He would come in to work when they were short staffed if he knew Maddy was working. He would stay later than scheduled if he knew Maddy was working. He did anything he could to be around her more and Maddy was none the wiser.

Maddy was so oblivious to Alec's interest in her. She had no idea that he fancied her and that her interest in Alec was mutual. She would even take pictues of the scheule in the break room when no one was looking to know when Alec was working with her and she'd get a little more dolled-up for work on those days. After the two hungout a few times, Alec decided to ask Maddy out on a date, but only one of them knew it was a date. Maddy thought it was a friend thing, she was very wrong.

After a few short months of dating, Alec had decied Maddy was the one and popped the question on December 28, 2020 and the rest is history.
